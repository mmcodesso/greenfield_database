# Reports Lens Hub

Use the lens-pack layer when you want a guided learning path instead of a full report inventory.

Each pack organizes existing reports into a business storyline:

- start with a lens and the core management questions
- move through a recommended report sequence
- use the discussion prompts and suggested analysis to interpret each report
- move next into the paired SQL or case page when you want a deeper drill-down

## Available Lens Packs

| Lens pack | Best use | Go to |
|---|---|---|
| Executive Overview | Broad company-level review of performance, position, cash, and working capital | [Executive Overview](executive-overview.md) |
| Commercial and Working Capital | Revenue quality, customer behavior, pricing, receivables, payables, and settlement timing | [Commercial and Working Capital](commercial-and-working-capital.md) |
| Operations and Risk | Capacity, supply reliability, planning pressure, workforce context, and controls | [Operations and Risk](operations-and-risk.md) |

## How the Learning Layer Fits the Report Section

- Use `Lens Packs` when you want guided interpretation and teaching prompts.
- Use `Report Library` when you want the full area-based report inventory.
- Use the SQL and case pages when students are ready to explain the logic behind the report results.
