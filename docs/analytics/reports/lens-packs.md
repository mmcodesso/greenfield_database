# Business Perspectives Hub

Business Perspectives organizes the report collection around the larger viewpoints management uses to read the company. Each perspective gathers related reports into one narrative so students can interpret the same business processes at a higher level instead of reading isolated report outputs one by one.

Each perspective still grows out of the process layer underneath. Commercial and working-capital questions come from O2C and P2P. Payroll and workforce questions come from time, attendance, pay, and liabilities. Operations and risk grows from planning, production, supply, and control signals. Executive overview ties the whole company together after those processes are already understood.

## How the Perspectives Grow Out of the Business

| Perspective | Main process backbone |
|---|---|
| [Executive Overview](executive-overview.md) | Close, statements, working capital, and the overall business system |
| [Commercial and Working Capital](commercial-and-working-capital.md) | O2C, P2P, settlement timing, and pricing |
| [Payroll and Workforce](payroll-perspective.md) | Payroll, attendance, labor support, liabilities, and control review |
| [Operations and Risk](operations-and-risk.md) | Manufacturing, supply reliability, planning pressure, workforce context, and controls |
